TAPSILOG

Eating tapsilog is all about the balance
of flavors. Tapsilog goes well with
chopped, fresh tomatoes, cucumber,
atchara (pickled green papaya) and
vinegar dip to90 balance the saltiness of
beef tapa, the richness of the fried egg,
and garlicky flavor from the fried rice.

PORK STEAK

A pork steak, also called Boston butt or
pork blade steak, is a steak cut from the
shoulder of the pig. Pork steaks are
mentioned in sources as far back as
1815, though without details about how
they were cut or how they were cooked.

SINIGANG NA HIPON

Sinigang na Hipon is a type of Filipino
sour soup, where in is used as
the main ingredient. This dish also
includes a variety of vegetables such as
daikon radish, snake beans, okra, and
eggplant. This dish is best served
during cold weather. It is best enjoyed
hot with a cup of white rice.
