Health Benefits of Cycling

-Cycling can help to protect you
from serious diseases such as
stroke, heart attack, some cancers,
depression, diabetes, obesity and
arthritis.

-Riding a bike is healthy, fun and a
low-impact form of exercise for all
ages.

-Cycling is easy to fit into your daily
routine by riding to the shops, park,
school or work.
